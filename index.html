Get started

Three paths into JellyRoll, depending on who's doing the building.

Designers

This site is the most recent version of JellyRoll — the source of truth, not Figma or Confluence.

  • Component library — ready-made elements for new projects and refactors
  • Style guide — colors, type, spacing for visual consistency
  • Design principles — guidance for interaction and user flows
  • Streamline your workflow; smoother handoff to developers

Developers

Drop in the tokens and reach for variables.

<link rel="stylesheet" href="colors_and_type.css">
