bar states
Clean (grey · disabled buttons · "All changes saved") · Dirty (yellow tint + left stripe · enabled buttons · "You have unsaved changes") · Saving (spinner · disabled) · Saved (green check · auto-clears in 3s back to clean).
placement
Bottom of the form area. In a Drawer: bottom of the drawer body. In a full page: pins to the viewport bottom while form is scrolled. Always inside the form container, not floating over content.
confirm
Triggered when user tries to navigate away with unsaved changes (route change · close drawer · ⌘W). Headline phrased as a question. Buttons use positive phrasing — "Yes, save" / "No, don't save" — never "OK / Cancel".
autosave
For long-form editors, replace the bar with an autosave indicator ("Saved 2 min ago"). Sticky bar still appears when validation errors block save.
a11y
Bar state changes use aria-live="polite". The confirm dialog traps focus, default-focuses the safest action ("Yes, save"), Esc dismisses to "stay on page".
