When to use
Diverging scales encode a value that can be above or below a meaningful midpoint — engagement vs. baseline, sentiment vs. neutral, expected vs. actual. The neutral grey center anchors "no change". Magenta and cyan are the rainbow's two anchor poles, giving high contrast at both ends. Reserve red ↔ blue diverging for explicit "good / bad" framing (SLA hits/misses); use this magenta ↔ cyan variant for neutral two-tailed data.
