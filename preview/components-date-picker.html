trigger
Field with calendar icon trailing. Displays selected date(s) formatted per locale.
popover
Floats below the trigger. Single picker = one month. Range picker = two months side-by-side + preset sidebar.
presets
Range picker offers common selections (Today, Last 7 days, Last 30 days, This/Last month, Custom). Active preset highlighted Blue-100.
states
Today: outline ring · Selected: blue fill · Range: blue tint between endpoints, solid blue endpoints · Muted: previous/next month · Disabled: grey, no hover.
keyboard
Arrow keys move day-by-day · PgUp/PgDn month-by-month · Shift+PgUp/PgDn year · Enter apply · Esc close.
a11y
role="dialog" aria-modal="true" aria-label="Choose date". Grid: role="grid"; day buttons: role="gridcell" aria-selected aria-current="date".
