Anchored to its trigger (split-button caret, icon "More", dropdown button). Opens 2px below; flips above when it would clip the viewport. Width ≥ 200px, grows to the longest item.

Trigger carries aria-haspopup="menu" + aria-expanded. Roving focus with ↑/↓; Enter activates; Esc closes and returns focus to the trigger. Sits at --z-popover.
