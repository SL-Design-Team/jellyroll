shape
A masked ring rendered from a conic gradient (transparent → currentColor over 360°), with a round "head" dot at the seam. Ring thickness controlled by --sl-spin-thick.
motion
Single 1.1s linear rotation. The taper itself is static within the spinner — the gradient does the work, so motion stays smooth and one-speed. Respects prefers-reduced-motion (slows to 2.8s).
sizes
xs 12 · sm 16 · md 24 (default) · lg 40 · xl 64. Stroke widens at xs/sm for visibility.
color
Inherits currentColor — set color on the parent. Variants (--brand · --teal · --ai · --success · --warning · --danger · --subtle · --inverse) are shortcuts to common values.
placement
In button: sm size, replaces or precedes label. Disable button while loading. In field: sm trailing, replaces icon. Block: lg/xl centered with label text below for full-section loads.
a11y
Wrap in role="status" aria-live="polite" with an aria-label describing what's loading. Don't rely on visual motion alone — screen readers need text.
vs. skeleton
Use a Spinner for short, indeterminate waits (<3s) and inline contexts. Use Skeleton loader for layout-shaped loads where you can preview the content's structure.
