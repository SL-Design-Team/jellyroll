widths
480 narrow (forms, single-task) · 560 medium (default — most config) · 720 wide (rich content, two-column body)
chrome
Header 56px (Title 18/28 ExtraBold + close X) · Body padded 20/24 with scroll · Footer 64px (right-aligned, Secondary + Primary buttons)
scrim
--color-scrim · rgba(9, 30, 66, 0.54). Covers the workspace below the global header — header stays visible.
motion
Slide-in 240ms cubic-bezier(0.2, 0, 0, 1) · scrim fades in 200ms linear. Same out, reverse order.
dismiss
X click · Esc · scrim click. Don't auto-dismiss on outside click for destructive or in-progress forms — confirm first.
a11y
Focus trap inside drawer. Restore focus to trigger on close. role="dialog" aria-modal="true" aria-labelledby="…" on the drawer; aria-label="Close drawer" on the X.
with SnapGPT
When SnapGPT is open, the Drawer's right edge anchors to the SnapGPT panel's left edge (not the viewport). The scrim covers only the workspace area — SnapGPT stays uncovered and interactive so users can keep chatting while configuring. See Drawer + SnapGPT pattern card.
